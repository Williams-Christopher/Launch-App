LaunchApp

Find out what's going up!

LaunchApp is designed to let you know about upcoming rocket launches and let you explore the launch sites in a map.

Launch app uses these technologies and services:

  • APIs
    • LaunchLibrary
    • Google Maps Platform
  • HTML 5
  • CSS 3
  • Javascript
  • jQuery
  • LaunchApp icon sourced from dreamstale

Click anywhere to begin exploring!
